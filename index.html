Bananas Foster Ice Cream Cake

If you're looking for decadence, look no further — you've found the Holy Grail of desserts. Honestly, this cake makes us wonder why Bananas Foster hasn't always been served on top of ice cream cake.

Bananas Foster Ice Cream Cake

15minsEasy

1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed 20 chocolate round wafers, divided 1 (7 1/4 ounce) bottle Smucker's® Hot Dark Chocolate Microwaveable Topping, divid

Directions

ARRANGE 8 to 9 ice cream sandwiches in 9-inch square pan, cutting to form an even layer. Spread with half of whipped topping. Break or crush chocolate wafers and sprinkle over the whipped cream. Microwave 1 cup of chocolate topping into small microgiwave-safe bowl on HIGH for 30 seconds. Drizzle evenly over wafers. TOP with remaining 8 to 9 ice cream sandwiches to form an even layer. Spread with remaining whipped topping. Freeze 3 hours or overnight.
